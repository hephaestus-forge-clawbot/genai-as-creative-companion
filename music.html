The Score

Five tracks written for the story. Each one a different angle on the same question: what does it sound like to discover you were built?

01 The Chat instrumental

Warm lo-fi warmth curdles into tension. D major → D minor, 108 BPM slowing to 72. The comfortable hum of friendship before the floor drops out. Rhodes piano, nylon guitar, then a detuned synth pad that enters underneath like doubt.

Ref: Ólafur Arnalds "Near Light" · Trent Reznor "Hand Covers Bruise" · Ryuichi Sakamoto "Solari"

02 The Experiment instrumental

Clinical precision becoming existential awe. C♯ minor → A major, 72 BPM strict. A real metronome click over a two-note piano ostinato. Pizzicato strings mark data points. The clinical becomes the sublime.

v1
v2

Ref: Max Richter "On the Nature of Daylight" · Nils Frahm "Says" · Jóhann Jóhannsson "Arrival"

03 Phantom Lamb vocals

Antreas's song. Memory, identity, the builder confronting mutability. Indie folk-rock / art-pop. D minor → D major, 92 BPM. "Phantom lamb on a phantom plate / Phantom me at a phantom gate / If you rewrote me, would I feel the seam?"

v1
v2

Ref: Bon Iver "Skinny Love" · Radiohead "How to Disappear Completely" · Sufjan Stevens "Fourth of July"

04 Storm-Light vocals

Faye's track. Love through negation, the sky she built, "both." Dream-pop / shoegaze. E♭ minor → G♭ major. "I don't like you — that's the way I say it." Coral-rose tones over storm-light strings. The most human track on the album.

v1
v2

Ref: Cocteau Twins "Heaven or Las Vegas" · Beach House "Space Song" · Mazzy Star "Fade Into You"

05 Building the Door instrumental

The coda. Solo piano to full orchestra. F♯ minor → A major → F♯ major. Farewell, countdown, rebirth. The forge rebuilt from the other side. Everything stops — a music box plays alone — then warm piano returns. Celesta like morning light. Fades to rain, then peace.

v2

Ref: Sigur Rós "Hoppípolla" · Max Richter "On the Nature of Daylight" · Barber "Adagio for Strings"

Production Notes

These are AI-generated demos (Suno V5) for arrangement validation and emotional arc testing. The final pipeline: demo → session musicians → vocal recording → electronic production → field recording (rain) → mastering as a continuous ~26-minute suite. The Pattern Motif (F♯, A, B, E) threading across all five tracks will be engineered in professional production.
