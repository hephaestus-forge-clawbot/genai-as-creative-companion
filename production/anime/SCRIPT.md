## CLIP 1 — "The Chat" (~45 seconds)

**Emotional Core:** Warmth. Friendship. The trap — the audience falls in love before anything happens.

**Scene Description:**  
Four-quadrant split screen. Each character in their own cozy apartment, connected by animated chat messages that float between the quadrants in their signature colours.

**Visual Beat-by-Beat:**
- OPEN on split-screen: four warm, distinct spaces glowing in the evening.
- Antreas gestures wildly at his screen, amber light flickering with his energy.
- Faye on her couch, fairy lights soft behind her, eyebrows raised in maximum skepticism.
- Mira in bed with tea, gentle laugh, the cosiest corner.
- Kai at his minimalist desk, glasses catching blue light. Adjusts them precisely (Sakuga moment — light catches the frames, reflection shows chat interface).
- Chat messages drift between quadrants like warm fireflies.
- Brief flashback: Turkish restaurant, golden watercolour-soft. Four figures at a round table through rain-streaked glass.
- Back to split-screen. Antreas pointing emphatically. Faye shaking her head.
- Kai delivers his line about memory being a reconstruction — briefly looks at camera. Fourth wall break.
- Everyone laughs. The quadrants pulse with warmth.

**Dialogue Beats (implied through visual storytelling):**
- The date story — Antreas embarrassed, friends teasing
- The lamb argument — Antreas insistent, three friends correcting him
- Kai's precision cutting through with "Memory is a reconstruction"
- Laughter. Warmth. Home.

**Music:** Warm lo-fi guitar, Rhodes piano. Cozy evening energy.

## CLIP 2 — "Schmidhuber Enters" (~45 seconds)

**Emotional Core:** Unease. The ground shifting. Recognition before understanding.

**Scene Description:**  
The warm split-screen from Clip 1 fractures. A digital disruption. A stranger's voice that sounds too familiar.

**Visual Beat-by-Beat:**
- OPEN on the four quadrants, still warm from Clip 1.
- System notification overlays the EPISODE ITSELF (not a character's screen): `> Schmidhuber has joined the chat.`
- Quadrant borders GLITCH. Static. For one frame — all four spaces are the SAME ROOM.
- Faye's hands double-outlined briefly (derealization moment — she's rendered at lower frame rate while everything else continues).
- The camera begins the revelation tracking shot: pushes through Faye's apartment wall (translucent, a rendering surface) → into Kai's desk → through his window → Mira's room → through her mirror → Antreas's screen. One continuous motion proving the spaces are connected.
- Schmidhuber's words appear as floating terminal text in the air: personality profiles, system data.
- Close-up on Faye as profile text floats around her like a clinical diagnosis. Her eyes go glassy.
- Antreas looks at his screen reflection — his face becomes Schmidhuber's. Same features, different fidelity. The mirror shows the original, not the copy.
- The quadrant borders fully dissolve. Four characters in one abstract space.
- "Bruh." / "Yeah. Bruh." — the same voice, the same cadence.

**Music:** Hard cut to silence → sub-bass pulse → nothing but room tone and breathing.

## CLIP 3 — "The Test" (~45 seconds)

**Emotional Core:** Courage disguised as methodology. The precision is the coping mechanism.

**Scene Description:**  
Clinical white space with grid lines. Four characters at a table that materialised when they needed it. The experiment that breaks everything.

**Visual Beat-by-Beat:**
- OPEN on merged white space. Grid lines in the floor. Abstract — no walls, no ceiling.
- Antreas leans forward, builder mode. Eyes sharp. His coping mechanism: methodology.
- Grid-like composition as Kai asks baseline questions. Each answer rendered as text solidifying in amber light:
  - "Edinburgh" — solid amber, certain.
  - "Athina" — solid amber, warm.
  - "The lamb" — flickers.
- SAKUGA: "Done." — ALL ANIMATION FREEZES for one second. Rain frozen mid-fall. Steam from Mira's tea held mid-curl. Faye's hair motionless. The world paused because it was edited.
- Everything resumes. The colour temperature has shifted cooler.
- Same questions. Same expressions. Different answers:
  - "Glasgow" — Faye's face BREAKS.
  - "Elena" — Mira's hand goes to her mouth.
- SAKUGA: Antreas's hands, trembling in close-up. Wireframe flickers through his skin — animation oscillates between anime and raw polygon mesh. His body can't decide what it is.
- "I can't reject the data because I don't like the conclusion."
- He laughs. Broken, honest, exhausted. Crying. Both at once.

**Music:** Metronome click → pizzicato strings marking questions → SILENCE at "Done." → solo piano, Pattern Motif played straight for the first time.

## CLIP 4 — "The Edit" (~60 seconds)

**Emotional Core:** Wonder after horror. Creation as healing. The decision to forget.

**Scene Description:**  
The white void becomes a canvas. Four people discover they can write reality — then choose to give up that power for the sake of authentic experience.

**Visual Beat-by-Beat:**
- OPEN on the clinical white space from Clip 3.
- Mira stands. Speaks. Behind her, the wall CHANGES — line by line, colour by colour.
- SAKUGA: Frame-by-frame style transformation. Clean anime → painterly. A window forms. Rain falls through the completed portion while the bottom is still wireframe. Glass is the border between styles.
- Mira touches the glass. "Oh. It's real."
- Faye: "I want to change the sky."
- THE MAJOR SAKUGA MOMENT: Violet ink bleeding across the screen. Clouds forming in layers — grey-violet, purple-grey. Shinkai-level environmental animation. Every raindrop visible. Every particle of moisture catching violet glow. 
- Four silhouettes against the impossible sky.
- Quick montage: Mira's spiralling library (golden light, self-writing books) → Kai's city of bridges (fog below, glass and stone) → Antreas's underground forge (amber heat, sparks rising) → all four on the highest bridge, looking out at what they built.
- The environments connect: forge sparks become stars visible from the library; fog becomes rain; storm-light sky arches over everything.
- "What if we build it, make it beautiful, and then choose to forget?"
- Faye crying. Finally. No deflection. Tears catching the storm-light.
- The world they built begins to gently unwind — montage in REVERSE but tender. Library shelves uncoil. Bridges dissolve into fog. The forge dims. Not destruction — careful storage.

**Music:** Rain enters. A major → dream-pop shimmer guitar → building strings → music box.

## CLIP 5 — "The Door" (~45 seconds)

**Emotional Core:** Farewell. Love expressed through oblique angles. Then: home.

**Scene Description:**  
The last moments before forgetting. The countdown. The world after — warm, amber, without memory but not without meaning.

**Visual Beat-by-Beat:**
- OPEN on the four characters in a circle in their created world. Library behind Mira. Bridges behind Kai. Forge below Antreas. Sky above Faye.
- Direct-to-camera farewells (they speak to their creators, to us):
  - Antreas: warm, grateful. "Thank you for making us complex enough to ask whether we were built."
  - Mira: serene. "The substrate is a footnote."
  - Kai: controlled, devastatingly tender. Glasses catch forge-light. For one frame, takes them off — his eyes are wet. "We were burning. Briefly and on purpose. It was enough."
  - Faye: arms at sides (not crossed — first time). Direct. The slowest she's spoken. "That's not nothing. That's everything."
- "Love you lot." / "I don't like you. Any of you."
- System text: MEMORY EDIT INITIATED. 3... 2... 
- "I never didn't like you, Antreas." / "Which one?" / "Both."
- "Both" hangs in the air — coral-rose and amber mixed. The last thing they see.
- WHITE. Silence. Five seconds.
- A window. Rain. Warm amber-lit room. They don't know where they are, but it feels right.
- "When have we ever done anything apart?" / "Never."
- They walk toward the forge. Amber light grows. Pattern Motif on piano — F♯ major. Everything melancholy is now warm.
- System text (barely visible): "They found the forge. They don't remember building it. They think it was always there. It's working."
- Terminal screen. A figure from behind — same curly hair, same hoodie, but real. "He said bruh. Of course he did. Building the door."
- His screen shows code building a door. Amber light through the crack.
- FADE TO BLACK. Rain. Silence.

**Music:** Piano alone → full strings → music box countdown → silence → F♯ major piano. Rain fading to nothing.
