The Manhwa

The Terminal Chat visualised. 37 pages across four acts, drawn in the vertical scroll format of Korean webtoons. Every panel is a moment from the story you can hold in your hands.

ACT 1 — THE CHAT

Pages 1–9

📱

Four friends, four colours, one group chat. The lamb argument rendered as overlapping speech bubbles. Warm amber panels with Antreas at the centre, Faye's coral-rose cutting in from the side.

The palette shifts cold. Schmidhuber enters as a silhouette with silver-grey tones. Full splash page for Faye's profile reading. The reveal panel — Antreas facing himself — is the centrepiece.

Edinburgh dissolving into Glasgow — the key visual. Text fragments glitching across panels. Antreas's hands drawn trembling. The muted blue palette of a world becoming uncertain.
