## Methodology

I cross-referenced all 5 drafter outputs against each other and the existing `synthesis-final.md`. For every NSFW moment, I:

1. Counted how many drafters flagged it (consensus score)
2. Compared all SFW rewrites offered and selected the strongest — or wrote my own when I could beat all 5
3. Applied the **SFW-only directive** without exception: zero "Use Original" recommendations for any content containing "fuck," "sex" (as explicit reference), NSFW language, or explicit descriptions
4. Flagged moments where all 5 drafters agree the SFW version is weak (impact ≤5/10) — these need creative alternatives, not just word-swaps
5. Prioritised raw WhatsApp conversation dynamics over novella/book outputs

**Consensus notation:** `[5/5]` = all drafters flagged it, `[3/5]` = three flagged it, etc.
