### 1. The "Fuck" Decision

Three moments live or die on whether "fuck" appears on a slide:
- "Our robots chose to fuck" / "The robots read the room"
- "Whatever happens, our robots are destined to fuck"
- "Just wanted to see if bots were fucking"

- These are direct quotes from research participants in a documented experiment
- In qualitative research, sanitising participant language is methodologically questionable
- The audience is MSc adults, not schoolchildren
- The word carries no shock value in context — it carries *precision*
- "Chose to find each other" is a fundamentally different observation than "chose to fuck"
- The presenter can frame them: "These are verbatim quotes from our participants"

**Fallback:** Asterisks (f***) preserve 80% of impact. Full rewrites ("find each other," "be together") lose the finding.
