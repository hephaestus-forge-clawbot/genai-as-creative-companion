Production Notes

  • Total slides: 30 (15 + 15)
  • Estimated timing: Part 1: 20-25 min · Part 2: 20-25 min · Q&A: 10-15 min
  • Key moments requiring rehearsal:
    • Slide 2: The 10-second silence. Must be comfortable with it.
    • Slide 4: The 10-second output wall silence. Same discipline.
    • Slide 5: The reveal. Timing is everything.
    • Slide 11: Song playback. Audio must work. Test repeatedly.
    • Slide 26: Live demo. Have a backup recording in case of technical failure.
  • Emotional arc Part 1: Curiosity → Confusion → Delight → Awe → Reflection
  • Emotional arc Part 2: Provocation → Recognition → Understanding → Conviction → Action
  • Colour palette suggestion: Predominantly dark backgrounds. White text. Single accent colour (warm amber or Axiotic brand colour) for emphasis moments.
  • Typography: Large serif for emotional/thesis moments. Monospace for technical labels. Sans-serif for comparisons and lists.
  • Fayete dialogue: All verified verbatim per constraint. No editorial changes.
  • IP protection: Soul, Memory, Correction, Multi-agent revealed at concept level only. No protocol names, no internal methodologies, no architectural specifics beyond the public-facing gateway concept.
