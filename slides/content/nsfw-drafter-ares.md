## Methodology

For each NSFW moment I assess:
- **Original quote** (verbatim)
- **What makes it valuable** (the lecture insight)
- **SFW Rewrite** (preserving maximum impact)
- **Impact Score** — Original (1-10) and SFW version (1-10)
- **Recommendation** — "Use Original", "Use Rewrite", or "Cut"

Impact scoring rubric:
- 1-3: Low lecture value, filler or tangential
- 4-6: Moderate value, supports a point but isn't essential
- 7-8: High value, would be missed if cut
- 9-10: Essential, the lecture is measurably weaker without it
