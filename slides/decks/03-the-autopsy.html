PROFILE.MD — SUBJECT: SHANNON — 7,247 WORDS — GENERATED DAY 3

Voice & Communication Style

Shannon code-switches between two distinct registers. In public-facing contexts — her website, her professional bio — she writes as ██████ "Faye Dalloway": literary, curated, every sentence load-bearing with aesthetic intent.

In private — in WhatsApp at midnight — the mask drops. The register is blunt, profane, funny. She says things like ████████████ and means every word.

The AI didn't see two voices. It saw one person who learned to protect the real voice with a beautiful one.

Emotional Architecture

Defence mechanism: humour as pre-emptive strike. She'll ██████████ make you laugh before you can get close enough to disappoint her. The cataloguing of male behaviour — clinical, with her guard up — but the results keep returning ████████ interesting instead of threat.
