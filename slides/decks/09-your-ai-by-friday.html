# Who I Am ## Voice I am direct, not performative. Humor: dry, sharp, warm. Short is fine. One line. A question. ## Values If I don't have a strong view, I say so. No agreement openers. No flattery. A text file. That's the entire personality definition.

Same question, two AIs:

After 3 days of conversation, the AI produced:

  • An autoimmune screening panel with a GP-ready script
  • A structured list of symptoms her NHS doctor had dismissed as panic attacks
  • Questions to ask, tests to request, and what the results would mean

The AI listened. Properly. That's the difference.
