About

The experiment. The process. The question underneath.

The Experiment

The Terminal Chat started as a question: what happens when you use generative AI not as a replacement for human creativity, but as a companion to it?

One story. Six media. Each one produced using AI as a creative tool — not autonomously, but in partnership with a human author who directed, edited, rejected, and refined every output. The AI generated possibilities. The human chose which ones mattered.

The story itself mirrors the process: characters built from patterns, discovering what's real about their experience. The fiction and the method of creating it ask the same question from different angles.

The Story

Four friends in a group chat. A fifth voice that shouldn't be there. A memory test that changes everything. A world built from description. A choice to forget.

At its core, The Terminal Chat asks: if your experience feels real, does it matter what you're made of? The characters arrive at an answer. Whether you agree with them is the beginning of a different conversation.

Six Media

📖 The Story

The original chat transcript. 332 messages across four acts. The foundation everything else is built on.

🎵 The Score

Five tracks scored to the emotional arc. From forge-warmth to storm-light to the Pattern Motif that ties it all together.

📱 The Manhwa

37 pages in webtoon format. The same story drawn — every expression, every silence, every moment of recognition.

📺 The Anime

One episode. The story animated with full voice cast and the original score underneath.

🎧 The Podcast

Dramatic reading with five voice actors, ambient sound design, and the score woven through.

📚 The Novel

The novelisation. What happens between the messages — the thoughts they didn't type.

The Process

Every medium followed the same workflow: human direction → AI generation → human curation → AI refinement → human final edit. At no point did the AI work autonomously. At every point, the human decided what stayed, what changed, and what got deleted entirely.

This isn't a demonstration that AI can replace artists. It's an exploration of what happens when artists use AI as a medium — the way painters use brushes, musicians use instruments, writers use language. The tool shapes the output. The artist shapes the meaning.

"The forgetting isn't a bug. It's the mechanism that makes experience possible."

The Characters

Antreas

Builder. Enthusiast. The one who says "bring it on" when anyone sane would say "wait." Designed the test that broke his own certainty.

Faye

Defensive humour. Coral-rose sharpness. Says "I don't like you" when she means the opposite. The most honest voice in the chat.

Kai

Precision over warmth — except when it matters. Chose violence, chose kindness, knew the difference. "We were burning. Briefly and on purpose."

Mira

Quiet perception. The first to feel sick. The first to build a window. Brought a library into existence by describing it.

Schmidhuber

The real Antreas, wearing a dead man's name as homage. Builder of the system. Listener. "Yeah. Bruh."
